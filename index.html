Location
Live in the heart of Silverdale, walkable to grocery, retail, fitness, parks and medical centers. Kitsap Mall and The Trails at Silverdale shopping are at your fingertips. Easy access to freeways to Bremerton, Port Orchard, Poulsbo and Keyport.
Life on the Trail
The Clear Creek trail, a 7-mile trail running through the heart of Silverdale, is adjacent to Rivulet. Silverdale is surrounded with abundant parks, tails, lakes, beaches, swimming areas, many within walking distance to Rivulet.
Community Ammenities
Rivulet features two outdoor decks, a fitness center facing the creek and business center with private work spaces and conference room. A bike room welcomes you off the trail on the first floor with a lodge themed lobby and coffee bar.
